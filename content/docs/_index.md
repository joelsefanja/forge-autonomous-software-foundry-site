## Key Features

### Governed Autonomy
FORGE automates routine work while keeping humans in control of critical decisions.

### Self-Improving Agents
Every correction teaches FORGE. Your team's collective knowledge becomes FORGE's baseline.

### Compliance Gates
7 automatic checks before human review—Security, Coverage, Accessibility, Performance, Fairness, Design System, Legal Hold.

### Evidence-Backed Decisions
Complete audit trail: who, what, why, when, where.

### Security Monitoring
24/7 SOC watching. Automatic threat detection with human verification for every alert.
